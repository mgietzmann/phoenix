# Question: What conditions does prey require to aggregate?

## Current Question
> What conditions are required for plankton and nekton to aggregate in the pelagic?

---

## Orient

**Audience**
Marine biologists and ecologists; readers may not have specialist knowledge of pelagic oceanography and will need the picture painted.

**Purpose**
To establish the physical and biological prerequisites for prey aggregation — setting up the argument that prey distribution is patchy and ephemeral. This is the first step toward the needle-in-a-haystack framing: before showing that conditions are unstable, show what conditions are needed.
